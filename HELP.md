# Minibee Viewer - Help

A friendly guide to what everything does and where to find it. No jargon.

Minibee is a **text-and-chat** viewer for Second Life - it lets you log in, chat, message friends, manage land and groups, and get around by teleporting. It does **not** show the 3D world, your avatar, or inventory.

---

## Logging in

1. Enter your **username** (e.g. `firstname.lastname`, or just `firstname` for old accounts) and **password**.
2. Pick your **grid** (Second Life is "Agni"; the beta grid is "Aditi").
3. Tick **Remember me** to save your username and grid for next time. Your password is **never** saved.
4. If your account uses two-factor (2FA), you'll be asked for a code. Tick "remember this device" to skip it next time.

Use **Forget saved login** to wipe the saved username, grid, and any remembered 2FA on this device.

---

## Getting around

Down the side (or along the bottom on a phone) are the main tabs:

- **Chat** - local/nearby chat. Type in the box at the bottom and press Enter.
- **IM** - private conversations, group chats, and conference (multi-person) chats.
- **Events** - pop-ups from the world: script dialogs, permission requests, payments, teleport offers. Nothing here ever answers for you - you decide.
- **Buddies** - your friends list. Tap a friend for options (profile, IM, teleport).
- **Search** - find people, places, and groups.
- **Radar** - who's near you, how far, and how long their account has existed.
- **Map** - the world map; search a region, click to pick a spot, and teleport.
- **Land** - details about the parcel you're standing on.
- **Guide** - the Destination Guide: featured places you can teleport to.
- **Settings** - preferences and info about the viewer (that's this screen).

A dot or number on a tab means there's something new there.

---

## Chatting

- **Nearby chat** (Chat tab): everyone around you sees it. Links in chat are clickable - web links open in your browser, and place links show on the map.
- **Instant messages** (IM tab): private. Open one from a friend, a profile, or a search result. You can also start a **group chat** or a **conference** (pick several people). In the conference window, type in the filter box to find someone by name.
- **Typing** shows when the other person is writing (one-to-one chats).

---

## Friends, profiles, groups

- Open anyone's **profile** by tapping their name in chat, radar, search, or your buddies list. Profiles show their About text, picks, and groups.
- **Add a friend** or **offer a teleport** from their profile or the buddies menu.
- **Groups**: your own profile lists all your groups. Open a group to read its charter, **set your active title**, or **make it your active group** (the Activate button, top of the group profile).
- **Picks**: a profile's favourite places. Open one to see where it is, then **Show on map** or **Teleport** straight there.
- **Private notes**: the Notes tab keeps private notes about someone that only you can see - you can even keep notes on your own profile.

---

## Getting places (teleport)

- **Map tab**: type a region name (or paste a SLURL / map link), or click a spot, then **Teleport**.
- **Guide tab**: browse featured destinations and teleport with one tap.
- **From a friend**: offer or request a teleport from their profile/buddies menu.
- **Home**: the Teleport Home button takes you to your set home location.

If a teleport is in progress you'll see a short progress note.

---

## Money and status

The top bar shows your **name**, the **region** you're in, your **L$ balance**, and the local **Second Life time**. Payments you make or receive appear in the **Events** tab.

---

## Parcel music

When a parcel offers a music stream, a little speaker appears in the top bar. Click it to play or stop. You can set the volume and whether music auto-plays in **Settings**.

---

## Settings

Everything you can change lives in one place - the **Settings** sub-tab:

- **Theme** - light or dark.
- **Radar** - how far it looks, and whether to alert you to newcomers.
- **Buddies** - show only online friends.
- **Destination guide** - which feed to open by default.
- **Parcel music** - auto-play and volume.
- **Auto-reconnect** - if your connection drops, Minibee can quietly log back in for you. **Off by default**; turn it on here. Your password is kept safely in memory only for this, never written to disk.

Changes take effect everywhere immediately, and are remembered for next time.

Closing the window while you're logged in asks you to confirm - so you don't quit by accident.

---

## When the connection drops

If Second Life disconnects you, you'll see a **Connection lost** notice. You can still read your chat and IM history while offline; grid actions are paused until you log back in. Turn on **Auto-reconnect** (Settings) to have Minibee keep trying for you.

---

## Something not working?

- **Names show as codes for a moment** - they resolve a second or two after they appear. Normal.
- **No sound / music won't start** - check the volume and auto-play in Settings; some browsers need you to click play once.
- **Nothing updates / stuck** - try logging out and back in. If it persists, the About tab has a **Copy all** button with version and system details to share when reporting a problem.
